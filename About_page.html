About CivicConnect

Empowering Naidupeta Through Digital Services

Welcome to CivicConnect, the dedicated online portal of the Naidupeta Municipal Corporation. Built with the citizen in mind, CivicConnect is our commitment to fostering a seamless, transparent, and responsive interaction between the local government and the residents of Naidupeta.

Our aim is to simplify and expedite various civic processes, making it easier than ever for you to voice your concerns, seek necessary permissions, and stay informed about the latest municipal activities. We believe that an empowered citizenry is the cornerstone of a progressive community.

CivicConnect is more than just a website; it's a digital bridge designed to enhance public service delivery, promote accountability, and ensure that every citizen of Naidupeta has direct access to the municipal services they need.

What CivicConnect Offers You

Effortless Complaint Management

Report civic issues, and track the status of your complaints with ease. From sanitation to public infrastructure, your voice helps us improve our city.

Event Permission Requests

Planning a public event? Apply for necessary permissions online, streamline your process, and ensure your gatherings are conducted smoothly and legally.

Health Camp Approvals

Organizations and NGOs can apply for permissions to conduct vital health camps in the community, contributing to the well-being of Naidupeta residents.
